BACKGROUND-CLIP

The BACKGROUND-CLIP property specifies the painting area of the background
The property takes three different values as shown above
background-clip: border-box
(default) the background is painted to the outside edge of the border
background-clip: padding-box
The background is painted to the outside edge of the padding.
background-clip: content-box
The pink background extends only to the edge of the content-box.
Thus the background is painted within the content box.
